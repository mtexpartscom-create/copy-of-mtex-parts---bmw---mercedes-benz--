## Selected Design: **Premium Dark Automotive Corporate**

Chosen for its balance of premium feel, brand alignment with BMW/Mercedes-Benz heritage, and corporate credibility. The warm dark palette avoids the coldness of pure brutalism while maintaining the technical precision expected of an automotive specialist. Syne + Plus Jakarta Sans provides distinctive character without sacrificing readability.
